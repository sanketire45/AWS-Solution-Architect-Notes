Cognito — Cheat Sheet

Managed app identity.

User PoolsIdentity Pools
PurposeAuthentication (who are you)Authorization to AWS (what can you access)
Gives youUser directory, sign-in, JWT tokensTemporary AWS credentials (via IAM role)
Use forApp login, MFA, social/SAML federationLet users call AWS directly (e.g. own S3 prefix)
Don't confuse
Cognito = identity for your app's end-users. IAM Identity Center = SSO for your workforce into AWS accounts. Different audiences.
Cost: User Pools free under 50,000 monthly active users.
